A.    Design

In this part, we will detail out the existing landscape of Yelp.com. This would include the graph model of yelp, its attributes and business rules. We will be creating 2 types of pseudo database schemas, MongoDB and Neo4j.

 

Mongo DB:

Business Rules and Explanation:

The above graph model represents the amazon review system. It follows the below business
rules:

1.     Nodes represent entities. Following are the selected nodes for the Yelp model,

a.     Business

Businesses listed on Yelp.com. These can be restaurants, hotels, gyms, dentists, phone repairs, dry cleaning, massage parlors, etc. Businesses are the blue nodes visible in the model.

b.    User

Customers are individuals registered on Yelp. Users can view all businesses listed on Yelp and post reviews and tips on them. User nodes are highlighted in purple.

c.     Review

Review node represents the reviews posted on Yelp by customers/users. Review nodes are highlighted in green.

d.    Tip

Tips are comments posted by customers/users about businesses. This is not the same as review. It can be treated as extra info posted by users based on experiences. These nodes are highlighted in yellow.

e.     CheckIn

This node denotes the numbers of customers/users who checked into the businesses for each day of the week. Checkin nodes are highlighted in orange.

 

2.     A business can be listed only once on Yelp.

3.     A business can have multiple tips, reviews and checkins.

4.     Users can post zero or more tips and/or reviews on businesses.

5.     Attributes display the fields captured by each node.

For further analysis, we created a new collection of the obtained result called â€œtextbased_helpful_reviewsâ€. Creating an index on the field â€œtextâ€ (Text of review):

db.review_subset.aggregate([{$match:{$text:{$search:" better extremely right quality experience quick friendly adept suggest good useful best awesome super cool supercool lovely amazing great excellent nice glad love adore disappointed pathetic sad bad perfect wonderful variety staff help fresh delicious terrible horrific rude awful fantastic ambience"}}},{$sort:{score:{$meta:"textScore"}}},{$project:{reviewID:"$review_id", score: { $meta: "textScore" },_id:0}},{$out:"textbased_helpful_reviews"}])

 

Â 

We create a non-text based definition which accounts for the total number of â€˜usefulâ€™, â€˜funnyâ€™, â€˜coolâ€™ which are numerical values. The â€˜helpfulnessâ€™ is defined as the total number of useful, cool and funny comments a review has received. We also use an additional filter where we discard the reviews which have received extreme ratings (1 star & 5 star rated reviews) as â€˜perfectly goodâ€™ and â€˜perfectly badâ€™ reviews have little bearing on the actual â€˜helpfulnessâ€™ since experiences of different users are subjective and depend on various personal user preferences and moods.
